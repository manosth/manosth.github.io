Competitions

I only recently started participating in competitions, however I already have participated to a decent number of them, with surprisingly good results.

Google Hashcode 2017

Together with the team Veni Vidi Vsync we participated to IEEE's XTREME in October of 2017. We placed 154th in the world rankings (out of about 2500 teams).

Codechef's SNACKDOWN 2017

Together with the team Veni Vidi Vsync we participated to Codechef's SnackDown in July of 2017. We placed 615th in the pre-elimination round (the total participation was 21,038 teams).

Google Code Jam 2017

I participated in Google's Code Jam in April of 2016.

Google Hashcode 2017

Together with the team Veni Vidi Vsync we participated to Google's Hashcode in February of 2017. We achieved the highest score in our hub, the third highest score in Greece, and 170th place in the world rankings (out of about 3000 teams).
